FAQ

What is this?

Can't remember what class you have next? Wondering how long left until the bell rings? Then this is for you!

GHS Timetable is a project that tells you when the bell rings and can will also show you what classes you have (eventually™)!

You'll never have to memorise your timetable again! Perfect when you're just waiting for the lesson to end (or waiting for school to start).
