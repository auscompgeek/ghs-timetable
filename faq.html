FAQ

What is this?

Can't remember what class you have next? Wondering how long left until the bell rings? Then this is for you!

GHS Timetable is a project that tells you when the bell rings and can also show you what classes you have! You'll never have to memorise your timetable again! Perfect when you're just waiting for the lesson to end.
